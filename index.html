About Me

Originally a native of Louisiana, I moved to Houston nearly twenty years ago. For almost that entire time I've been employed with CenterPoint Energy, starting out as a meter reader but now working in the Major Underground Department as a high voltage cable splicer.

I've always held an interest in a lot of things over the years: music, cooking, astrophysics, comics and history just to name a few. I now seek to bolster my interst in something by learning about web development at the UT Coding Bootcamp.
